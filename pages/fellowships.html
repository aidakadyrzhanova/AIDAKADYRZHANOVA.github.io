Fellowships

Don Lavoie Fellowship - Mercatus Center, George Mason University


The Don Lavoie Fellowship is a competitive, renewable, and online fellowship program open to students from any discipline who are interested in studying key ideas in political economy and learning how to utilize these ideas in academic and policy research. Fellows join a network of Mercatus scholars who are conducting and engaging with cutting edge research in contemporary political economy.
